# What is Islam?

Islam is an Arabic word meaning **"peace, security, and submission."** As a religious term, Islam means **complete submission to Allah** and **obedience to His commands.**

## The Meaning of Islam

The word Islam derives from the Arabic root **"salama"** and has the following meanings:

- **Peace**: Inner peace and social harmony
- **Security**: Being under Allah's protection
- **Submission**: Complete surrender to Allah's will
- **Salvation**: Happiness in this world and the hereafter

## The Core Message of Islam

The fundamental message of Islam is simple and clear:

> **"La ilaha illa Allah"** - There is no god but Allah.

This statement represents the essence of Islam:
- **Tawhid**: The unity and uniqueness of Allah
- **Worship**: Devotion only to Allah
- **Lifestyle**: Living according to Allah's commands

## The Chain of Prophets

Islam is the final link in the chain of prophets sent throughout history:

### Important Prophets
- **Adam (peace be upon him)**: The first human and prophet
- **Noah (peace be upon him)**: The prophet of the flood
- **Abraham (peace be upon him)**: The friend of Allah (Khalilullah)
- **Moses (peace be upon him)**: The prophet who brought the Torah
- **Jesus (peace be upon him)**: The prophet who brought the Gospel
- **Muhammad (peace be upon him)**: The final prophet

## The Final Prophet: Muhammad (peace be upon him)

Muhammad (peace be upon him) is the final prophet of Islam:

### His Characteristics
- **Born in 571 CE** in Mecca
- **Began his prophetic mission at age 40**
- **Delivered the Quran** to humanity
- **Lived an exemplary life** teaching Islam through his actions

### His Mission
- To convey Allah's final message
- To guide humanity to the right path
- To spread justice and mercy
- To establish an exemplary community

## How to Enter Islam?

To enter Islam, it is sufficient to recite the **Shahada (Declaration of Faith)**:

### The Shahada
**Arabic**: "Ashhadu an la ilaha illa Allah wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan abduhu wa rasuluh"

**English**: "I bear witness that there is no god but Allah, and I bear witness that Muhammad is His servant and messenger."

### The Meaning of Shahada
1. **Unity of Allah**: The one and only Creator and God
2. **Muhammad's Prophethood**: Believing he is the final messenger
3. **Submission**: Accepting with the heart and practicing in life

## The Fundamental Principles of Islam

### 1. Tawhid (Unity of Allah)
- Allah is one and unique
- He has no partners
- He is the Creator and Owner of everything

### 2. Risalah (Prophethood)
- Allah sent prophets to humanity
- Muhammad is the final prophet
- The Quran is the final book

### 3. Akhirah (The Hereafter)
- This world is temporary
- There is resurrection after death
- Paradise and Hell are real

## The Five Pillars of Islam

1. **Shahada**: Declaring your faith
2. **Salah**: Praying five times daily
3. **Zakah**: Giving a portion of wealth to charity
4. **Sawm**: Fasting during the month of Ramadan
5. **Hajj**: Pilgrimage to Mecca for those who are able

## Conclusion

Islam is the final and universal message sent to humanity. As a simple, understandable, and practical religion, Islam aims for human happiness in both this world and the hereafter.

For our brothers and sisters who have newly embraced Islam, this is just the beginning. Learning and living Islam is a beautiful lifelong journey.
