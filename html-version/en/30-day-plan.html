30-Day Plan

30-Day Plan

10 min read
Beginner

A 30-day systematic learning plan for new Muslims. Learn and apply the fundamentals of Islam step by step each day.

How the Plan Works

Week 1: Fundamentals

Basics of Islam, pillars of faith, and fundamental worship

Week 2: Prayer and Worship

Learning prayer, ablution, and daily worship practices

Week 3: Daily Life

Islamic lifestyle, ethics, and social relationships

Week 4: Deepening

Quran reading, supplication learning, and Islamic culture

Week 1: Fundamentals (Days 1-7)

Day 1: What is Islam?

  • Meaning and basic principles of Islam
  • Learning the Shahada (Declaration of Faith)
  • What it means to be Muslim

Day 2: Faith in Allah

  • Allah's existence and oneness
  • Beautiful Names of Allah (Asma ul-Husna)
  • Concept of Tawhid (Monotheism)

Day 3: Faith in Angels

  • Characteristics of angels
  • Important angels and their duties
  • Gabriel, Michael, Israfil, Azrael

Day 4: Faith in Books

  • Divine books: Torah, Gospel, Psalms
  • Importance of the Holy Quran
  • Preservation of the Quran

Day 5: Faith in Prophets

  • Mission of prophets
  • Life of Prophet Muhammad (SAW)
  • Other important prophets

Day 6: Faith in Day of Judgment

  • Death and life in the grave
  • Day of Resurrection
  • Paradise and Hell

Day 7: Faith in Destiny

  • Concepts of fate and destiny
  • Human will and responsibility
  • Trust and patience

Week 2: Prayer and Worship (Days 8-14)

Day 8: Cleanliness and Ablution

  • Cleanliness in Islam
  • Steps of performing ablution
  • Ghusl (full body ablution)

Day 9: Prayer Times

  • Five daily prayers
  • Learning prayer times
  • Finding the Qibla direction

Day 10: Prayer Positions

  • Standing, bowing, prostrating
  • Prayer movements
  • Correct postures

Day 11: Prayer Recitations

  • Surah Al-Fatiha
  • Glorifications and supplications
  • Opening supplication

Day 12: First Prayer Experience

  • Performing morning prayer
  • Step-by-step practice
  • Mistakes and corrections

Day 13: Other Prayers

  • Noon, afternoon, sunset, night prayers
  • Characteristics of each prayer
  • Number of units in each prayer

Day 14: Friday Prayer

  • Importance of Friday prayer
  • Etiquette of going to mosque
  • Congregational prayer

Week 3: Daily Life (Days 15-21)

Day 15: Halal and Haram

  • Permissible foods
  • Forbidden things
  • Doubtful situations

Day 16: Family Relations

  • Rights of parents
  • Rights of spouse
  • Child upbringing

Day 17: Social Relations

  • Neighborhood rights
  • Friendship
  • Social responsibilities

Day 18: Work Ethics

  • Honest trade
  • Trust
  • Justice

Day 19: Zakat and Charity

  • Importance of Zakat
  • Calculating Zakat
  • Giving charity

Day 20: Fasting

  • Importance of Ramadan fasting
  • Etiquette of fasting
  • Iftar and Suhur

Day 21: Hajj and Umrah

  • Importance of Hajj
  • Hajj rituals
  • Umrah pilgrimage

Week 4: Deepening (Days 22-30)

Day 22: Quran Reading

  • Arabic letters
  • Simple chapters
  • Reading translation

Day 23: Learning Supplications

  • Daily supplications
  • Meal supplications
  • Travel supplications

Day 24: Dhikr and Tasbih

  • Morning and evening dhikr
  • Using prayer beads
  • Seeking forgiveness

Day 25: Islamic History

  • Life of Prophet Muhammad
  • First Muslims
  • Spread of Islam

Day 26: Islamic Festivals

  • Eid al-Fitr
  • Eid al-Adha
  • Mawlid celebrations

Day 27: Muslim Community

  • Mosque culture
  • Islamic education
  • Community cooperation

Day 28: Personal Development

  • Self-discipline
  • Patience and gratitude
  • Spiritual growth

Day 29: Future Plans

  • Continuing Islamic education
  • Community integration
  • Family planning

Day 30: Evaluation

  • 30-day assessment
  • Topics learned
  • Continuation plan

Daily Recommendations

Daily Tasks

  • Read the day's topic (15-20 min)
  • Practice what you learn
  • Note your questions
  • Don't forget to pray

Important Notes

  • Don't rush, be patient
  • Ask about things you don't understand
  • Visit your local mosque
  • Make Muslim friends

Conclusion

This 30-day plan is designed to help you learn the fundamentals of Islam. By learning a little each day, gradually embrace Islamic life step by step.

Remember that the learning process is not limited to 30 days. This plan is just a beginning. Continue learning throughout your life.
